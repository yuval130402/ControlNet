Open the BIOS (Restart the System and press the F2 key multiple times).
In the system, BIOS navigate to Power Management and Wake on LAN/WLAN.
Activate the Option you wish to use. 

In order to use WOL, 
you should set the Windows-side Wake-On LAN settings on the NIC that is currently CONNECTED.
setting:
1. "Allow the device to wake the computer" to CHECKED
2. "Only allow a magic packet to wake the computer" to CHECKED
3. "Energy Efficient Ethernet" to OFF
4. Fast Startup to DISABLED (Windows 8-10 only)
